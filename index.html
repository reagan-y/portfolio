Ecomaid
Ecomaid is a local cleaning company based in Roanoke, VA. Their mission is to provide a housekeeping service that not only gives their customers an excellent clean but also uses practices that are better for their clients and the planet. They wanted their branding to feel earthy and organic while also appealing to their younger client base, so we decided to go in a clean, minimalist direction while focusing on organic textures and colors.
Mighty Green Rebrand
Mighty Green is a local lawncare company in Alabama and is a part of the Turf Masters Brands Family. They were looking for a brand refresh that included an updated logo and some print media for Mighty Green. Their main concern about the original logo is that it wasn't condusive to one- or two-color printing, so the priority was to create a logo that would function with many different colorways. With the agreed upon logo redesign, two different creative directions were developed for print media including yard signs, mailers, and doorhangers.
Organizational Resource Guide (.ORG)
The Organizational Resource guide was a collaborative project with designers Ariadna Perez, Tre Venable, and Kylie Woods. The goal for this publication was to bring a sense of accessibility and joy to research around local and international organizations.
Open House Branding and Identity
The concept behind this Open House exhibition was centered around the transitional space of a home being moved out of. While spearheading the development of the logo, a cardboard box was chosen as a representation of both moving and of transitional space. As the box goes through a cycle of being broken down and built back up, the layers build up to create an abstracted and layered form of a house. Chaotic imagery and colors were also used to signify the varigated nature of the work being shown at the exhibition.
